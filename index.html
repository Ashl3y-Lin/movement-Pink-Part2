Observations

  • -> Why doesn't my robot's wheel stop spinning?
  • Check if you are using degrees instead of rotation!

  • -> Why is does your move different than mine?
  • That's because of
  • * The type of ground/surface (the more slippery, the less degrees you need)
  • * Maybe your robot design is a little different than mine (every robot move differently)
  • * The battery of the robot (Remember to charge your robot!)
